Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation means new ideas that solve problems and improve our lives.

Sustainability

Sustainability means using resources wisely to protect nature for the future.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure means roads, buildings, and systems that support daily life smoothly.

Empowerment

Empowerment means giving people tools and confidence to achieve their goals.

The Vision of Future


Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh 2.0 is a vision of transformation where we move beyond just economic growth and focus on human development, social equality, and environmental sustainability. It’s about using the power of technology, innovation, and social entrepreneurship to build a future where poverty no longer exists, and everyone has the opportunity to thrive. We need to ensure that growth is inclusive, and prosperity benefits all sections of society.
